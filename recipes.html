Interesting US Coffee consumption facts!

myfriendscoffee.com team has spent over 300 hours on research about coffee, below are a few of their findings:

  1. In 2019, 64% of Americans aged 18 and over drank coffee every day.
  2. Americans rank 25th for coffee consumption per capita, with an average consumption of 4.2 kg per person per year.
  3. The USA ranks 11th among the countries with the highest caffeine consumption, with a rate of 200 mg per person per day.
  4. The average U.S. coffee drinker consumes 2.7 cups per day, with the average size of a coffee cup measuring 9 ounces.
  5. More than 150 million Americans drink about 400 million cups of coffee per day or more than 140 billion cups per year.
  6. Coffee consumption in the U.S. in millions of 60-kg bags.
  7. What time of day do Americans prefer to drink coffee? (Beverages Town):
    • In the morning – 65%
    • In the evening – 30%
    • Other – 5%
  8. The most popular methods of making coffee in the USA:
    • Drip coffee maker – 45%
    • Espresso machine – 12%
    • Other – 30%
  9. The most searched coffee drinks in the USA:
    1. Caramel Macchiato
    2. Flat White
    3. Cappucino
    4. Cold Brew
    5. Latte Macchiato
